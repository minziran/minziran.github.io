CV / Scholar / Github

I have focused on artifical intelligence (AI) technologies since 2015, when I have worked on face recognition for Bachelor thesis.

During my Master's period (2015.09-2018.07) in Tsinghua (research-oriented, 3 years length program), I have continued working on face recognition topics, where I devote myself in machine learning especially deep learning.

I have joint Vanderbilt University since 2018.08 as a Ph.D. student, where I mainly focus on medical imaging and informatics (specifically, lung cancer risk estimation). My research topics include multi-modal learning, time-serial learning, missing data and anomaly detection. As first author, I have won RFW All-conference Best Student Paper Finalist twice (2020, 2021) in SPIE Medical Imaging, and C.F. Chen Best Paper at Vanderbilt University.
